AI-Powered Video Narration

Creating narration for videos is tedious — writing scripts, syncing timing, recording voiceovers. Narrator does it all automatically. Drop in a video and get perfectly timed narration with professional audio, in minutes instead of hours.

Download for Free View on GitHub

Six-Step Wizard Workflow

From raw video to polished narration — Narrator guides you through every step.

Step 1

Project Setup

Import video files via drag-and-drop or record your screen directly with the built-in recorder.

Step 2

Edit Video

Trim, split, reorder clips, adjust playback speed, and drop unwanted frames before narration.

Step 3

Configuration

Choose your AI provider, narration style, language, and attach context documents.

Step 4

Processing

Automatic frame extraction, document processing, and AI narration generation with real-time progress.

Step 5

Review & Edit

Preview narration synced to video, edit scripts per-segment, adjust timing, and preview voices before export.

Step 6

Export

Export narrated video with burned subtitles, generate TTS audio via ElevenLabs or Azure, and save scripts in SRT, VTT, JSON, Markdown, and more.
